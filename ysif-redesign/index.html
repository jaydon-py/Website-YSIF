01 Who we are

Practical investment
experience.

Developing investment judgement through research, analysis and structured evaluation.

Based at the University of York, YSIF is fully operated, organised and funded by students. Members gain direct experience in fundamental research, valuation and portfolio construction.

The fund meets weekly to consider company research and review investment assumptions. Members contribute to sector coverage, portfolio strategy and risk management.

Alongside the fund's investment activity, we run a structured seminar series to improve students' understanding of investing and support their progression towards careers in finance.

02 Education and progression

Structured learning
and responsibility.

A six-week financial education series provides members with a practical foundation in investment analysis.

The programme introduces equity research, financial analysis, valuation and the principles of portfolio management. Following the series, participants work in groups to research and present a selected group of equities to the fund for formal review.

Continued participation gives members the opportunity to take on greater analytical, editorial and leadership responsibility. The structure is intended to develop relevant professional skills and support career progression within finance and investment management.

03 Our approach

Our investment
process.

Our process is based on detailed research, constructive scrutiny and collective accountability.

01

Fundamental research

We study businesses from the ground up—industry dynamics, financial quality, competitive position and long-term opportunity.

02

Investment review

Members assess each investment thesis, review the principal risks and evaluate whether its conclusions are supported by the available evidence.

03

Analyst evaluation

Investment proposals are evaluated by our analysts, with consideration given to the supporting research, valuation, principal risks and suitability for the portfolio.

Important information

YSIF is an independent student organisation based at the University of York. It is not legally affiliated with, operated by, or endorsed by the University of York. YSIF is a student-led educational society operated for academic and extracurricular purposes only. The society does not provide regulated investment services, financial advice, or investment management services. Membership fees may be used to support society activities, including collectively managed educational investing activities conducted by members of the society. Membership does not constitute ownership of portfolio assets, entitlement to investment returns, or participation in a collective investment scheme under Section 235 of the Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 (FSMA). Membership fees are non-refundable and cannot be redeemed for any proportional share of the society portfolio or investment gains. The society is not authorised or regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA). All activities are conducted collectively by members for educational purposes only. Capital invested by the society is at risk.
