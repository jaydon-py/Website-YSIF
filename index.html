York Student Investment Fund

Who we are.

Based in the University of York, YSIF is a student-led fund dedicated to managing an investment portfolio, with the aim of providing members with practical experience and valuable insight into the world of investing. YSIF is fully operated, organised and funded by students - with no alumni backing.

About

YSIF is a student-led investment fund based at the University of York. Our members develop their skills in fundamental equity research, valuation, and portfolio construction.

We meet bi-weekly to pitch, debate, and vote on proposals. Every member plays an active role in the fund's strategy, from sector coverage to risk management, giving them experience that goes beyond the lecture theatre.
